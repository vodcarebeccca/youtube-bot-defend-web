YouTube Channel Protection

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 8, 2024

Introduction

YT Bot Defend ("we", "our", or "the Application") is a desktop application designed to help YouTube content creators protect their channels from spam and gambling-related comments (judol). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

1. Google Account Information

When you connect your Google/YouTube account, we access:

  • Your YouTube channel ID
  • Your channel name and profile picture
  • Your email address (for authentication only)

2. YouTube Data

With your explicit permission, we access:

  • Live chat messages in your streams
  • Comments on your videos
  • List of moderators in your live chats
  • Your video list (to select which videos to moderate)

3. Application Usage Data

We collect locally on your device:

  • Detection statistics (spam detected, deleted, etc.)
  • Application settings and preferences
  • Bot account configurations

How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses:
  • Spam Detection: Analyze messages to identify spam, judol, and toxic content
  • Moderation Actions: Delete spam and ban offenders (only on your channel)
  • Statistics: Show you reports of moderation activity
We DO NOT:
  • Store your YouTube data on external servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Sell your personal information
  • Access channels you don't own
  • Take actions without your explicit configuration

Data Storage

Local Storage Only

All data is stored locally on your computer:

  • OAuth tokens (encrypted)
  • Application settings
  • Detection logs
  • Bot configurations
No Cloud Storage: We do not upload your YouTube data to any cloud servers. All processing happens locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

Google/YouTube API

We use the YouTube Data API v3 to authenticate your account and perform moderation actions. Your use of Google services is also governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

AI Detection Services

We use AI services (Gemini, Mistral, Groq) to analyze message content for spam detection. Only the message text is sent for analysis - no personal information about the sender is shared.

Your Rights

1. Access Your Data

View all data stored by the Application in the local database files.

2. Delete Your Data

  • Remove your connected accounts at any time
  • Delete all local data by uninstalling the Application
  • Clear detection logs from the Application settings

3. Revoke Access

Revoke YouTube access at any time:

  1. Go to https://myaccount.google.com/permissions
  2. Find "YT Bot Defend"
  3. Click "Remove Access"

Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication
  • Local encryption of sensitive tokens
  • No transmission of personal data to external servers
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all API calls

Children's Privacy

YT Bot Defend is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and showing a notification in the Application.
